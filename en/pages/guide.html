Step 1. Open Mines BOT in Telegram

1. Open bot link

Use the button above or open the link from our Telegram channel.

2. Press Start

Tap Start to initialize the bot conversation.

3. Choose language

Select the interface language you prefer.

Step 2. Register on 1win

1. Go via bot link

Use the registration button inside the bot.

2. Fill registration form

Enter phone/e-mail, password, and account currency correctly.

3. Account is ready

Return to the bot and confirm your registration status.

Step 3. Top Up Deposit

1. Open top-up section

In your 1win account, press the top-up button.

2. Choose payment method

Select card, wallet, or another option and complete payment.

3. Add a starting amount

Deposit an amount you are comfortable testing with.

Step 4. Launch Mines BOT App

1. Return to Telegram bot

After deposit, return to the bot chat.

2. Tap “Application”

Press “Application” / “Open App” to open the mini-app.

3. Select Mines game

Choose Mines mode inside the mini-app.

4. Play with signals

Keep 1win Mines and the bot mini-app open in parallel and follow highlighted safe cells.
